Guess It or Miss It Math Quiz Game Instructions

Welcome to the Guess It or Miss It Math Quiz Game!

Instructions:

  1. Two players take turns to answer math questions.
  2. Each correct answer earns you a point.
  3. The first player to reach 3 points wins the game.
  4. If you answer incorrectly, the turn goes to the other player.
  5. Click the "Submit" button to check your answer.
  6. Click the "Reset" button to start a new game.
